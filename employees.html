Passionate people behind sustainable fashion

Learn about the team

Aaron is a dedicated team member at EcoStyle, where he works as a sustainability coordinator. With a passion for both fashion and environmental conservation, Aaron plays a key role in ensuring that EcoStyle's products meet the highest standards of sustainability. His expertise in eco-friendly materials and production processes helps the brand source and create clothing that is not only stylish but also minimizes environmental impact.

Aaron is committed to reducing waste in the fashion industry and finding innovative ways to make EcoStyle’s operations even more sustainable. When he's not working on product development or sourcing new materials, Aaron enjoys educating others on the importance of ethical fashion and is always looking for ways to inspire the team and EcoStyle’s customers to make more sustainable choices.
